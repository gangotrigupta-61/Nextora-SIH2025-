Interview

A curated list of free, real-world resources to help you ace your next technical interview.

Free Resources

MIT OpenCourseWare - Introduction to Algorithms

A highly-regarded course from MIT that covers fundamental algorithms and data structures in depth. Includes video lectures and assignments.

Platform: MIT OpenCourseWare

GeeksforGeeks - Data Structures Tutorials

A very popular and comprehensive reference for data structures, with detailed explanations and code examples in multiple languages.

Platform: GeeksforGeeks

LeetCode

The go-to platform for coding interview practice, with thousands of problems and a vibrant community.

Platform: LeetCode

HackerRank

Another popular platform for practicing coding challenges, with a wide variety of problems and skill tracks.

Platform: HackerRank

Grokking System Design

A popular YouTube series that provides a step-by-step approach to solving common system design interview questions.

Platform: YouTube

Front-End Interview Handbook

A comprehensive collection of front-end interview questions and answers, covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more.

Platform: Free Website

Front-end Developer Interview Questions

A huge list of questions covering a wide range of topics from general knowledge to specific frameworks like React and Vue.

Platform: GitHub

The STAR Method

A video guide on the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result), a powerful technique for answering behavioral questions effectively.

Platform: YouTube

Tell Me About Yourself

A video from "The Star Method" channel on how to answer the most common opening question in a job interview effectively.

Platform: YouTube

FreeCodeCamp - Interview Prep Guide

A massive, free guide with articles, videos, and practice problems covering all the key concepts for a coding interview.

Platform: FreeCodeCamp

Tech Interview Handbook - Mock Interviews

A guide to finding and doing mock interviews, which is one of the best ways to practice for the real thing.

Platform: Free Website

Front-End Developer Roadmap

A detailed, step-by-step guide to becoming a front-end developer, including all the skills and technologies you need to learn.

Platform: roadmap.sh

Back-End Developer Roadmap

A comprehensive guide for learning the skills needed to become a back-end developer, from basic concepts to advanced architecture.

Platform: roadmap.sh

Full-Stack Developer Roadmap

A guide to navigating the complex world of full-stack development, covering both front-end and back-end technologies.

Platform: roadmap.sh
