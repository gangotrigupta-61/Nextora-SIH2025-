About the Project

We are a passionate team participating in the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2025. Our project focuses on building an AI Career Advisor Platform that empowers students and professionals to take smarter career decisions.

Our Mission

To provide AI-driven career guidance that is accessible, personalized, and impactful.

Our Vision

To become the go-to digital career advisor platform for every student in India.

Project Highlights

AI-Powered Guidance

Smart recommendations tailored to each user’s skills and interests.

Mentorship

Connect with mentors for real-world career insights and support.

Career Roadmaps

Step-by-step visual paths for education and job roles.

Aptitude Tests

Evaluate strengths through interactive tests across multiple domains.
